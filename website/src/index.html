Analysis of Twitter Messages related to drugs

Data from Twitter API and Drugs.com was analysed using NLP techniques.

Percentage of positive Tweets

People tweet positive messages about drugs related to diseases with minor symptoms, like minor aches and high blood pressure and cholesterol and less positive about drugs related to more severe diseases such as MS and cancer. In addition we can see that the over the counter drugs are tweeted equally for positive and negative messages.

Percentage of Tweets that try to sell online/cheap drugs

We see that all of the drugs with high percentage of tweets are prescription drugs, and to less severe symptoms diseases. I speculate that people with more severe symptoms will go to the doctor and get a prescription. Most of the drugs with low percentage are over the counter drugs and prescription drugs for severe symptoms.
